Frequently Asked Questions

When will SongDeck launch on Android?
We're working hard on it. Join the waitlist on our homepage and you'll be the first to know the moment it goes live.
Is the Pro upgrade a one-time payment?
Yes — ₹349 once, yours forever. No subscriptions, no recurring fees. Your Pro access follows your account across all platforms.
Does SongDeck work without internet?
Fully. Your songs are stored on your device first. SongDeck syncs to the cloud when you're online, but works completely offline.
How do I add my band members?
Create a band in the app and share the 6-character invite code with your bandmates. They join instantly by entering the code.
Can I delete my account and data?
Yes. You can request account deletion at any time by emailing us. We'll delete all your data within 30 days.
What instruments does SongDeck support?
Currently Guitar and Keys, with full transpose and capo support. More instrument types are planned for future updates.
