What's Inside

Built for the stage,
not the studio.

📚
Song Library

Store chords, lyrics, key, capo, and tempo. Everything you need for every song, right at your fingertips.

🎯
Smart Setlists

Build setlists with drag-and-drop reordering. Manage personal and band setlists separately.

🎹
Live Transpose

Instantly transpose any song across all 12 keys mid-performance. No more scribbled notes on paper.

📡
Offline First

No WiFi at the venue? No problem. SongDeck works fully offline and syncs when you're back online.

🌙
Stage-Ready UI

Dark mode, adjustable font size, and full-screen performance view. Designed for low-light stages.

☁️
Cloud Sync

Your songs live on your device first and sync to the cloud automatically. Never lose your library.

Band Collaboration

Your whole band,
one setlist.

Create a band, share songs with your team, and manage who can edit. Members get view-only access — the creator stays in control.

  • → Lock songs so members can only view, not edit
  • → Members can copy band songs to their personal library
  • → Shared band setlists everyone can follow live
